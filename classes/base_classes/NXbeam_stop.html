3.3.1.26. NXbeam_stop¶

Status:

base class, extends NXcomponent

Description:

A device that blocks the beam completely, usually to protect a detector. ...

A device that blocks the beam completely, usually to protect a detector.

Beamstops and their positions are important for SANS and SAXS experiments.
